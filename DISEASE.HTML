DISEASES

Cold and Flu

Description- In contrast to the flu, a common cold can be caused by many different types of viruses. The condition is generally harmless and symptoms usually resolve within two weeks.
Spread by- By airborne respiratory droplets (coughs or sneezes).
By touching a contaminated surface (blanket or doorknob).
Symptoms- Symptoms include a runny nose, sneezing and congestion. High fever or severe symptoms are reasons to see a doctor, especially in children.
People also experience some variation in symptoms like-
Pain areas:  muscles
Cough: can be with phlegm
Nasal: congestion, runny nose, sneezing, loss of smell, redness, or post-nasal drip
Cure- Most people recover on their own within two weeks. Over-the-counter products and home remedies can help control symptoms
As it is usually vulnerable to children, Pediatrician can be a specialist for it’s treatment. .

Tonsillitis ( Tonsils )

Description-an inflammation of the two oval-shaped pads of tissue at the back of the throat.
Tonsillitis is usually caused by a viral infection but can be from a bacterial infection.
Symptoms-symptoms include sore throat, difficulty swallowing and tender lymph nodes.
People also experience other symptoms-
Pain areas: in the ear
Pain circumstances: can occur while swallowing
Whole body: chills, fatigue, fever, or malaise
Cure-
Self-care - Tea with honey, salt water gargle, throat lozenge. These all soothes sore throats.
Medication- Antibiotics, penicillin etc.
Surgery-Tonsillectomy, surgical removal of the tonsils.
Specialist- Pediatric ENT's .

Diarrhea 

Description- it is usually caused by a virus. Less frequently, it can be a sign of another disorder, such as inflammatory bowel disease or irritable bowel syndrome.
Symptoms-loose, watery stools occur more frequently than usual.
Cure-
Self care - Replacing lost fluids with an oral rehydration solution (ORS) may help in preventing dehydration.
If the condition hasn’t improved within 2 days, seek a doctor immediately.
Specialist- gastroenterologist .

Conjunctivitis (pink eye)

Description- It can be caused by allergies or a bacterial or viral infection. Conjunctivitis can be extremely contagious and is spread by contact with eye secretions from someone who is infected.
Symptoms- symptoms include redness, itching and tearing of the eyes. It can also lead to discharge or crusting around the eyes. Pain can also be experienced in the eye.
Cure-
Self care- cold compress can help in dulling the sensation in the pain.
Medication- antibiotics and Steroid can stop the growth of bacteria. Allergic conjunctivitis can be treated with antihistamines
Specialist- ophthalmologist.

Diabetes 

Description- the high level of glucose or sugar present in the blood result in diabetes.
There are 2 types of diabetes-
Type 1 - A chronic condition in which the pancreas produces little or no insulin. It typically appears in adolescence.
Symptoms- include increased thirst, frequent urination, hunger, fatigue and blurred vision.
Cure-
Self care- regular sugar level check-up and maintaining the sugar/glucose level. Diabetic diet must be followed which consist of less sugar. Keeping a track of carbohydrate being consumed.
Medication- insulin, it maintain the sugar level. Dietary supplement can also be helpful.
Type 2 - With type 2 diabetes, the body either doesn't produce enough insulin, or it resists insulin.
Symptoms- people may experience –
Excessive hunger, weight gain or loss, frequent urination.
Cure-
Self care- physical workout can be included in daily routine. Improving the cardiovascular health. A diabetic diet must be followed.
Medication- Anti-diabetic medicines help in controlling the sugar/glucose level.
As it is hormonal disease, Endocrinologist can be helpful.

Malaria

Description-A disease caused by a plasmodium parasite, transmitted by the bite of female anopheles mosquitoes.
Spread by-By animal or insect bites or stings. By blood products (unclean needles or unscreened blood).
Symptoms-symptoms are chills, fever and sweating, usually occurring a few weeks after being bitten. People also experience pain in abdomen, night sweats, fast heart rate, fatigue etc.
Cure-
Medication-Antimalarial drugs, antibiotics, antiparasitic are helpful.
Specialist- Infectious Disease Doctor .

Typhoid

Description- it is a bacterial infection which spread through contaminated water and food products. It requires a medical diagnosis.
Symptoms- They include high fever, headache, stomach pain, weakness, vomiting and loose stools. Headache, muscle weakness, rash with small red dots, skin rash, or weight loss can also be common.
Cure-
Supportive care- IV fluids can be ejected directly into the veins for medication. The most effective treatment for typhoid is antibiotics.
Specialist- In typhoid as well Infectious Disease Doctor can do the treatment.

Tubercolosis

Description-it is a bacterial disease which mainly affects the lungs but can be vital. The bacteria that cause tuberculosis are spread when an infected person coughs or sneezes.
Symptoms- Patients don’t really experience some special symptoms when it gets infected by the bacteria but when they occur they include night sweats, fever, weight loss, cough etc.
Cure-
Medication- the treatment is tedious and long-course, but the multiple antibiotics are used as a medication.
Specialist- as this disease is indirectly related to lungs, pulmonologist can be helpful. .

Chicken Pox

Description-it is a highly transferable viral infection which causes an itchy, blister-like rash on the skin. Chickenpox is highly vulnerable to those who haven't had the disease or been vaccinated against it.
Symptoms- the most common symptom is an itchy, blister-like rash on the skin. Scab, ulcers, or red spots can also be spotted on the skin. Loss of appetite can also be one of the symptoms.
Cure-
Self care- oatmeal bath soothes the irritated skin.
Medication- Vaccines, analgesic, antihistamine relieve pain and stop the allergic reactions.
Specialist-pediatrician can be helpful.

Covid 19

Description-Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. The virus spreads mainly between people who are in close contact with each other, typically within 1 meter (short-range).
Symptoms- Most common symptom include fever, tiredness, dry cough. Serious symptoms are difficulty in breathing, chest pain, loss of speech.
Cure-
Self care- isolate into a separate room, take rest and drink a lot of a fluid, monitor temperature regularly.
Medication- Vaccines is the most effective against covid right now.
Specialist- there is no specialist of covid-19 till now.

Alzheimer’s Disease

Description- A progressive disease that eradicates memory and other important mental functions. The brain cell and their connection deteriorate and die. It is a common disease.
Symptoms- memory loss and confusion are the main symptoms. mental decline, difficulty thinking and understanding, delusion, disorientation, forgetfulness, making things up can be other cognitive symptoms. Depression, hallucination, or paranoia can be the psychological symptoms.
Cure-
Self care- Physical workout can be helpful in maintaining the mental level as well.
Medication- cognition-enhancing medication can improvise the mood and the mental level.
Specialist- Geriatrician, neurologist and psychiatrist can be helpful. .

Pneumonia

Description- Infection that inflames air sacs in one or both lungs, which may fill with fluid. It can be a life-threatening disease.
Symptoms- Symptoms include a cough with phlegm or pus, fever, chills and difficulty breathing. Patient may also experience a sharp pain in chest, fatigue, loss of appetite and fast breathing.
Cure-
Supportive care – oxygen therapy, oral rehydration therapy and IV fluids can a affective against it.
Medication- Antibiotics and penicillin can stop the growth of bacteria.

Stroke

Description- it occurs due to interruption in blood supply of the brain, preventing the brain tissue from getting oxygen and nutrients. Brain cells begin to die.
Symptoms- Symptoms of stroke include trouble walking, speaking and understanding, as well as paralysis or numbness of the face, arm or leg. Patient also may experience visual problems, speech problems and facial problems.
Cure-
Supportive care – Cardiac monitoring, maintain and checking the heart rate.
Medication- Antihypertensive drug, ACE inhibitor and statin etc, can be helpful in lowering blood pressure, relaxing blood vessel and decrease the amount of cholesterol being produced by the liver.
Surgery- Carotid endarterectomy is the removal of plague build up in carotid arteries.
Specialist- Neurologist, neurosurgeon and occupational therapist are recommended for the patient.

Coronary heart disease

Description- Damage in the heart’s major vessels. The usual cause is the build-up of plaque. This causes coronary arteries to narrow, limiting blood flow to the heart.
Symptoms- Coronary heart disease can range from no symptoms, to chest pain, to a heart attack. Patients also experience light-headedness or sweating in whole body.
Cure-
Self care- the patient is expected to workout regularly for 20-30 mins. A low fat diet must be strictly followed.
Medication- Beta blocker can be used which slows the heart rate and decreases the blood pressure.
Calcium channel blocker relaxes the blood vessels. Statin can also be used in this diseases as well.
Surgery-Coronary artery bypass surgery restores the flow of blood in the veins by using a healthy vein to bypass the blocked vein.
Specialist- Cardiologist is recommended for the patient.

High blood pressure (hypertension)

Description- It is the condition in the force of blood applied on the artery walls in too high.Usually hypertension is defined as blood pressure above 140/90, and is considered severe if the pressure is above 180/120.
Symptoms- This disease typically do not have a symptom. But over time, if untreated, it can cause health conditions, such as heart disease and stroke.
Cure-
Self care- Physical workouts, low sodium diet, home blood pressure monitors are helpful.
Medication- Diuretic, vasodilator and antihypertensive drug are recommended. They all collectively help in preventing the life-threatening conditions.
Specialist- Cardiologist and nephrologist are recommended for the patient. .

Piles

Description-swollen and inflamed veins in the rectum and anus that cause discomfort and bleeding. It is usually caused by straining during bowel movements, obesity or pregnancy.
Symptoms-Discomfort is the most common symptom, especially during bowel movements or when sitting. Other symptoms include itching and bleeding. People also experience some pain in the rectum.
Cure-
Self care- high fiber diet must be followed. Ice packs and cold compress dull the sensation of pain.
Medication- steroid help in modifying hormonal effects and also reducing the inflammation. Dietary supplement can also be taken.
There are other medical procedures as well to help the patient, some of those are cauterization,
Sclerotherapy, rubber band litigation etc.
Specialist- PCP, Primary Care Provider .

HIV

Description-HIV (human immunodeficiency virus) is a virus that attacks the body's immune system. If HIV is not treated, it can lead to AIDS (acquired immunodeficiency syndrome). The virus can be transmitted through contact with infected blood, semen or vaginal fluids.
Symptom- Within a few weeks of HIV infection, flu-like symptoms such as fever, sore throat and fatigue can occur.
Then the disease is usually asymptomatic until it progresses to AIDS. AIDS symptoms include weight loss, fever or night sweats, fatigue and recurrent infections.
Cure No cure exists for AIDS, but strict adherence to antiretroviral regimens (ARVs) can slow down the progressivity of the disease.
Medication- HIV antiviral can suppress the progression of the infection also reduces the risk of affecting others.
Specialist- Infectious Disease Doctor .

Asthma

Description-A condition in which a person's airways become inflamed, narrow and swell and produce extra mucus, which makes it difficult to breathe. In the beginning asthma can be minor but it might lead to life threatening attacks.
Symptoms- it can cause difficulty in breathing, chest pain, cough etc. People also experience anxiety, early awakening, throat irritation.
Cure-
Self care-if the patient smokes, he/she has to quit smoking.
Supportive care– Oxygen therapy can help the patient.
Medication- Bronchodilator can open the airways of the lungs for smooth breathing. Anti-inflammatory can prevent the swelling in the joints and tissues.
Specialist- Pulmonologist .

Measles

Description- it is a viral infection, it can be vital for small children. The disease spreads through the air by respiratory droplets produced from coughing or sneezing.
Symptoms- the symptom are not visible or noticeable until 10-14 days of the infection. They include cough, runny nose, inflamed eyes, sore throat, fever and a red, blotchy skin rash. Patient can also have pain in the muscles.
Cure-
Medication- analgesic can give relieve. Measles can be easily prevented by vaccines.
Specialist- Pediatrician, Infectious Disease Doctor.

Lung Cancer

Description- cancer which occur in the lungs. Causes of lung cancer include smoking, second-hand smoke, exposure to certain toxins and family history. Two major types of lung cancer are non-small cell lung cancer and small cell lung cancer.
Symptoms- Symptoms include a cough (often with blood), chest pain, wheezing and weight loss. These symptoms are noticeable when cancer has progressed. The most common symptom is pain in the chest and ribs and in the back as well.
Cure- The treatment depends totally on the stage of cancer.
Self-care- quit smoking, if the patient does.
Medication- Chemotherapy, it is used to kill the cancer cells. Targeted therapy, it slows cancer cell growth.
Surgery- Pulmonary Lobectomy, it is the removal of the lobe of the lung. Wedge Resection, it is the removal of the triangular piece of the tissue from the lung.
Medical Procedure-- Radiation Therapy is also used to kill abnormal cells, it uses x-rays and high energy-rays.
Supportive-care – Palliative Care, it helps the patient in adjusting with cancer. It also somehow improves quality of life.
Specialist-Oncologist, Cardiothoracic Surgeon

Breast Cancer

Description- A cancer that forms in the cells of the breasts. Breast cancer can occur in women and rarely in men.
Symptoms- Symptoms of breast cancer include a lump in the breast, bloody discharge from the nipple and changes in the shape or texture of the nipple or breast. Patient also experience discomfort, redness, swollen lymph nodes etc.
Cure- It’s treatment also depends on the stage.
Medication- Estrogen Modulator, emulate the effect of estrogen on several tissues. Bone health, strengthens and build bones. Chemotherapy is also practiced.
Medical Procedure- Teletherapy, it uses x-rays and high energy rays to kill the cancer cells and tumor.
Surgery- Mammaplasty, it is the process in which the size or breast is reduced or increased to remake the breast. Lymph node dissection, it the removal of lymph node. Lumpectomy, removal of the tumor in the breast. Mastectomy, removal of all or some of the breast.
Specialist- Oncologist, Plastic surgeon, Palliative Medicine( focuses on improving the quality life of the patient.
